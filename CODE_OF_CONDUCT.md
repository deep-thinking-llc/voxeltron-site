# Code of Conduct

Voxeltron is built for operators who need clear thinking under pressure. We want the project space to feel the same way.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but focus on facts and evidence.
- Keep technical disagreement professional.
- Share reproducible details when reporting bugs or incidents.
- Protect private information, credentials, and customer data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or demeaning language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

Questions about conduct or moderation can be sent to `conduct@voxeltron.dev`.
